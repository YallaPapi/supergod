# 3 Sample Content Packs (Fast Proof Versions)

Use these as fixed-scope offers for first clients. Keep delivery speed high and scope tight.

---

## Pack 1: Local Service "Booked Jobs" Pack

Best for: medspa, dental, roofing, plumbing, gym, salon, cleaning.

### Offer
- `12 short videos/month` (15-30 sec)
- `12 captions` with CTA to DM or booking link
- `3 posting slots/week` automated
- `1 monthly content refresh call` (20 min)

### Client Promise (safe wording)
- "We will ship 12 posts/month consistently and optimize for DM/bookings based on actual response data."

### Production flow
1. Intake: services, offers, reviews, city names, before/after media.
2. i2v: generate hooks + b-roll style clips + text overlays.
3. Editing pass: brand colors, service keywords, CTA variants.
4. Geelark/Instagram scheduler: queue 4 weeks.
5. Weekly optimization: swap weak hooks, keep top performers.

### 30-day KPI targets
- Posting consistency: `>= 90% of planned posts published`
- Reach lift vs previous 30 days: `+25% to +80%`
- DM starts from content CTA: `10 to 40`
- Booking-link clicks: `15 to 60`

### Starter pricing
- Pilot: `$600 setup + $900 month 1`
- Standard: `$1,500/month`

## Pack 2: Coach/Consultant "Authority Engine" Pack

Best for: business coach, fitness coach, agency owner, career consultant.

### Offer
- `20 short videos/month`
- `8 carousel/text posts/month`
- `daily weekday posting` automated
- `comment-seeding prompts` for engagement

### Client Promise (safe wording)
- "We turn your raw ideas into a daily authority stream so prospects see you everywhere with consistent messaging."

### Production flow
1. Capture 45-60 min founder voice notes or Q&A.
2. i2v pipeline: split into hook/topic angles.
3. Generate 4 content pillars:
   - mistakes
   - frameworks
   - client stories
   - contrarian takes
4. Schedule to Instagram; adapt selected posts for Reddit threads.
5. Weekly review: keep top 20% formats, replace underperformers.

### 30-day KPI targets
- Posting consistency: `>= 95%`
- Profile visits lift: `+30% to +120%`
- Inbound lead DMs: `20 to 80`
- Qualified calls booked from content: `4 to 20`

### Starter pricing
- Pilot: `$800 setup + $1,200 month 1`
- Standard: `$2,000 to $3,000/month`

## Pack 3: Ecommerce "UGC Conversion Loop" Pack

Best for: DTC brands with 1-5 hero products.

### Offer
- `24 short videos/month` (problem/solution, comparison, testimonial style)
- `12 static support creatives/month`
- `6 posting days/week` automated
- `creative angle testing sheet` updated weekly

### Client Promise (safe wording)
- "We produce and publish a high-volume UGC-style creative stream that continuously tests new hooks and buying objections."

### Production flow
1. Intake: product USP, reviews, competitor ads, FAQs.
2. i2v: generate variant scripts and visual treatments.
3. Build batches by funnel stage:
   - awareness hooks
   - objection handling
   - proof clips
   - offer urgency
4. Auto-schedule to Instagram; repurpose for Reddit-native educational posts where allowed.
5. Weekly winner extraction for paid/organic reuse.

### 30-day KPI targets
- Posting consistency: `>= 90%`
- CTR to product page from profile/link posts: `+15% to +50%`
- Saves/shares increase: `+20% to +100%`
- First-order attributed sessions from social: measurable lift vs baseline

### Starter pricing
- Pilot: `$1,000 setup + $1,500 month 1`
- Standard: `$2,500 to $4,000/month`

## Evidence Capture Checklist (for all packs)

For each client, archive proof weekly:
- i2v generation batch IDs
- scheduler queue screenshot with timestamps
- post URLs and publish status
- engagement screenshot at 24h/72h
- DM/bookings/clicks export

This turns fulfillment activity into sales proof quickly, without needing long historical case studies.
