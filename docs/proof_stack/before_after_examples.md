# Before/After Style Examples (Show, Don’t Explain)

Use these as "proof snapshots" in DMs, Looms, and proposals.

## Example 1: Weak Promo Post -> Lead-Gen Post (Local Service)

### Before
- Visual: generic stock clip + logo
- Caption: "We offer quality dental services. Call us."
- CTA: none
- Expected issue: low retention, no reason to act

### After
- Hook text (0-2 sec): "3 signs you need a dentist this month (not next year)"
- Body: 3 quick pain-point bullets with local context
- CTA: "DM `SMILE` for same-week checkup openings"
- Caption includes geo + offer + proof line

### Why it wins
- Specific pain-led hook
- Direct DM keyword CTA
- Local relevance + urgency

## Example 2: Generic Motivation Clip -> Authority Clip (Coach)

### Before
- Script: "Stay consistent and never give up."
- Visual: random gym footage
- CTA: "Follow for more"
- Expected issue: low trust, interchangeable message

### After
- Hook: "Most coaches stay broke because their offer is too broad."
- Body framework:
  1. Diagnose mistake
  2. Show one clear fix
  3. Give mini-proof from client scenario
- CTA: "Comment `OFFER` and I’ll send the 5-line repositioning template."

### Why it wins
- Contrarian hook stops scroll
- Teaches one usable method
- Comment CTA drives visible engagement proof

---

## Example 3: Flat Product Post -> UGC Objection Handler (Ecommerce)

### Before
- Visual: product spin only
- Caption: "New drop available now"
- CTA: "Shop link in bio"
- Expected issue: low conversion intent

### After
- Hook: "If your skin reacts to [common ingredient], watch this before buying."
- Body:
  - objection callout
  - demo/use-case
  - social proof line from review
- CTA: "Save this and use code `TRY10` before Friday."

### Why it wins
- Starts from buyer objection
- Adds proof + deadline
- Save/share behavior increases distribution

---

## Proof Presentation Format (one slide each)

For each before/after pair, show:
1. Original post screenshot
2. Rebuilt post script + caption
3. First 72h metrics delta:
   - views
   - watch-through
   - saves/shares
   - DMs/clicks

Even small initial wins (for example, 1.5x watch-through or +8 DMs) are enough to close early clients when shown clearly.
