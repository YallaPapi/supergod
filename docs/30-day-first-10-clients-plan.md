# 30-Day Plan: Close First 10 Paying Clients

## Outcome and math (so KPIs are defensible)
- Target by Day 30: `10 paying clients`.
- Core channel for speed: `direct outbound` (Instagram + Reddit + email/DM), with content as trust layer.
- Working funnel assumptions (conservative but achievable for service offers with personalized outreach):
  - `600 targeted outbound touches / 30 days` (~20/day average).
  - `8% positive reply rate` -> `48 positive replies`.
  - `50% booked-call rate from positives` -> `24 sales calls`.
  - `42% close rate from calls` -> `10 clients`.
- Secondary assist from inbound content: `1-3 extra opportunities` (buffer).

## Offer architecture (used throughout)
- Core offer: "Done-for-you content creation + posting" for 30 days.
- Entry offer (fast yes): 14-day pilot with clear deliverables and fixed price.
- Upsell path: monthly retainer after pilot.
- Risk reversal: performance checkpoint after first 7 days of pilot.

## KPI dashboard (track daily)
- Activity KPIs: outbound touches sent, follow-ups sent, posts published, comments/engagement actions.
- Funnel KPIs: positive replies, booked calls, calls held, proposals sent, closed-won clients, cash collected.
- Quality KPIs: reply rate %, show rate %, close rate %, CAC time per closed client.

## Weekly checkpoints (non-negotiable)
- Day 7: Are replies <5%? Fix targeting and opener before increasing volume.
- Day 14: Are closes <2 total? Narrow ICP and force pilot-only CTA.
- Day 21: Are closes <5 total? Shift 70% effort to follow-up + calls, 30% new outreach.
- Day 28: Are closes <9 total? Pause content production extras; run pure close sprint.

## Owner operating cadence (daily)
- 60 min: lead sourcing and list hygiene.
- 90 min: new outbound DMs/messages.
- 45 min: follow-ups.
- 45 min: sales calls/proposals.
- 45 min: fulfillment/proof capture for active clients.
- 30 min: KPI logging and next-day adjustment.

## Stoplight rules (fast decisions)
- Green: reply rate >=8%, show rate >=70%, close rate >=40% -> keep volume steady.
- Yellow: one metric below target for 2 days -> iterate script/qualification.
- Red: two metrics below target for 3 days -> pivot niche angle + offer framing same day.
