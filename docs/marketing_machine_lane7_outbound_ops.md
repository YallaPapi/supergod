## 5) Funnel Math Targets (Evidence-Based Operating Ranges)
Use conservative B2B service outbound assumptions:
- Positive reply rate target: 3-8%
- Call booking rate target (from contact): 1-3%
- Close rate target (from calls): 20-35%

Implication for first 10 clients:
- If close rate is 25%, need ~40 sales calls.
- If booking rate is 2%, need ~2,000 contacts.
- At 50 new contacts/day, this is ~40 working days.

Optimization goal:
- Raise booking rate to 3% and close to 30% to reach 10 clients in ~22-28 working days.
